About Me

I'm a web developer living in the Raleigh-Durham area of North Carolina with my wife and three wonderful kids.

Building standards-based websites has been my profession since mid-2008, when a family member suggested that I might be interested in web development because I was "good with computers." Since then, I've devoured every resource I can get my hands on in order to learn more about my craft.

I've worked in the agency world as well as higher education, and have integrated code with over a dozen content management systems. Like a chef, I aim to produce quality results no matter what ingredients are placed before me.
